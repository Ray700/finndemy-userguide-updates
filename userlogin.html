Finndemy User Guide - Learners

Account Registration & Login

How to Create Your Finndemy Account

1. Visit finndemy.com.

2. Click the "Sign Up" button on the top right.

3. Fill in your details, including username, email, and password.

4. Verify your email from the link sent to your inbox.

5. Log in using your credentials.

How to Enroll in a Course

1. Browse courses from the “Explore Courses” menu or use the search bar.

2. Click “Enroll Now” for your chosen course.

3. For paid courses, add to cart and proceed to checkout.

4. Complete payment and access your course immediately.

Forgot Password? How to Reset

1. From the login page, click “Lost your Password?”

2. Enter your registered email to receive a reset link.

3. Follow the email instructions to create a new password.
